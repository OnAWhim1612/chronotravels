Chrono Travels - Mission 1

Mission 1: The Pink Artefact Maze
Description: You find yourself in a mysterious ancient maze where the legendary Pink Artefact is rumored to be hidden. Your mission is to navigate through the labyrinth and find the Pink Artefact. Beware of traps and dead-ends along the way. Use your wits and spatial awareness to complete this challenging maze game and retrieve the artifact!
